## Overview

Recif integrates with MLflow GenAI to provide a comprehensive evaluation pipeline for your agents. Every release can be gated by quality scores, ensuring that only agents that meet your standards reach production.

> [!NOTE] This page is under active development. Full content coming soon. See [Eval-Driven Releases](/docs/concepts/eval-driven-releases) for the conceptual overview.

## What You'll Learn

- Setting up MLflow for agent evaluation
- Available scorers (correctness, relevance, faithfulness, and 11 more)
- Creating golden datasets for consistent testing
- Configuring quality gates and minimum scores
- Feedback-to-dataset loops for continuous improvement
- Viewing evaluation results in the dashboard
