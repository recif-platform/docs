## Overview

Knowledge bases in Recif are powered by Maree, a separate ingestion product that follows an evaluation-first approach. Documents are ingested into a staging index, evaluated against golden datasets, and only promoted to production if they meet quality thresholds.

- Pluggable pipeline: Source, Processor, Transformer, Store

- Evaluation-first ingestion with staging and promotion
- Embedding model configuration (Ollama, OpenAI, Vertex AI)
- Assigning knowledge bases to agents
